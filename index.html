IVL Rulebook

1 Calendar and Match scheduling

The league will start March 29th and will end around the first week of June. A total of 18 rounds will be played (teams will play each others twice)

1.1 Match Scheduling

Every team will play against any other team twice (home and away).

2 rounds will be played every week (every team will play 2 other teams), every two weeks there will be a ’’short week’’ with only 1 round. In detail:

  • Month 29/03-25/04: 2 rounds + 2 rounds + 1 round + 2 rounds
  • Month 26/04-23/05: 2 rounds + 2 rounds + 1 round + 2 rounds
  • Month 24/05-06/06: 2 rounds + 2 rounds

1.2 Couples Division

Every team is REQUIRED to show up with a couple at the designed day/time and in the specified room.

It is not required to speficy which of the two couples will play that day, all is needed is that the couple show up in time in the room.

The couples must be registered in #sign-up, it is not allowed to trade players between the couples of the teams.

1.2.1 Example 1

On ’Round 1’ (March 29-30) it is specified on the schedule:

Team3 vs Team5 at 21:30 in ROOM1

If Team3 is made of couples A and B and Team5 is made of couples C and D, then the games will be:

Thursday 08/04 - Couple A vs Couple C at 21:30 in ROOM1

Friday 09/04 - Couple B vs Couple D at 21:30 in ROOM1

1.2.2 Example 2

On ’Round 4’ (April 08-09) it is specified on the schedule:

Team3 vs Team9 at 22:30 in ROOM2

If Team3 is made of couples A and B and Team9 is made of couples N and M, then the games will be:

Thursday 08/04 - Couple A vs Couple N at 22:30 in ROOM2

Friday 09/04 - Couple **B vs Couple M at 22:30 in ROOM2

2 Postpone / Anticipate a match

Depending on one’s needs, it is possible to pospone or anticipate a match (considering the above example, you could change the schedule of the single match A V C or both matches as well).

To do so, you need to find an agreement, in private, with the opponents, and only then leave a message in the #postpone-and-anticipate-match channel pinging the @Referee role.

How much time do you have to tell the @Referee about the schedule change?

  • In case of postponement —> 1h30m before the original time
  • In case of anticipation —> 1h30 before the new agreed time

You can postpone/anticipate for no more than 3 days (unless there’s exceptional motivation to communicate to the @league admins).

3 Match

3.1 Showing up to the match

It is required to show up on time in the room at the given time.

A player can be at max 15 minutes late, after which the refree will give DEFWIN (Default 2-0, 7-0 7-0 win) to the team that regularly showed up.

3.2 Playing the match

It is required playing in our rooms (#link-room) and an official @Referee needs to be present.

The couple to reach 7 points first will win 1 set (no advantage)

The first couple to win 2 sets will win the match.

The match will be considered regularly played only if a replay was recorded and if the two couples have played the match fully, without leaving the room.

In case of a player or a couple leaving a DEFWIN will be awarded to the opponents.

If the match is unplayable because of lag from one or more players, the designated @Referee can decide to suspend the match and pospone it; the couples will be free to agree to postpone the match following the rules above in Postponing/anticipating a match, or finding an agreement with the referee in room to play later (es: 30m/1h/2h later).

3.3 Lag and Check

In case of obvious lag, the @Referee can spontaneously decide to replay the point and take the point away from the team who had scored.

In case the lag is not obvious is nto evident to the @Referee, it is possible, once the play is over, to ask for a Check.

The Check consists of sending a screenshot in the #screen-check channel, in which it’s visible the ping graph (the graph with the ping on the left of the chat), the map and the players, the score of the match and the date/time of the pc.

At this point, the @Referee will decide if they want to give the Check and replay the action (taking away the point) or validate it and restart the match.

In case the Check is validated, the team will be awarded another Check during the set, otherwise it can’t be called for the rest of the set

4 Rankings

In the general rankings you will find the 10 teams (NOT couples) ordered by points earned.

The points will be assigned separately to the two couples based on the results of their match, and then summed.

In case of parity of points, the following critieria will apply:

  1. Wins
  2. Set won
  3. Set Difference
  4. Points for
  5. Points Difference

4.1 Example

Team FASSA BORTOLO: og-pac and Dangerxx-lelouch

The couple og-pac WINS the match against the opponent couple 2-0 and earns 3 points

The couple Dangerxx-lelouch LOSES the match against the opponent couple 1-2 and earns 1 point

The team FASSA BORTOLO will have earned a total of 4 points (3+1), 3 set wins (2+1) and 2 lost sets (0+2)

5 Final Phases

The Final Phases will be played at the end of the regular season schedule and the first 4 classified teams will play.

It consists in a knock-out mini-tournament (semifinals and finals) played in 1 or 2 days.

In those matches the couples will NOT be separated anymore: There will be a single match in which substitutions are accepted and whichever couple formed from the 4 players of the teams can play.

Every player will however be Required to play at least 1 full set

The first team to reach 7 points (no advantages for the semi-finals, with advantages for the final) will win 1 set, the first team to reach 3 sets wins will win the match.

6 Team/Couple Withdradwal

In case of a couple withdrawing from the tournament, the other couple of the team can call 2 substituting players within 2 rounds.

Otherwise, every match the whitdrawing couple has played or would have played will be considered DEFLOSS (2-9, 7-0 7-0), while the other couple can keep playing their matches (WILL NOT be able to play the games that the whitdrawng team was supposed to play).

If the entire team (both couples) whitrdaws from the league, the @League admin will propose the Free agents first and eventually unregistered players later, to form a new team that will keep the results and the ranking of the whitdrawing team.

In case a new team cannot be formed, every match played or to play of the whitdrawing team will be considered DEFLOSS (2-0, 7-0 7-0)

7 Free agents

Free agents are the players registered to the league but that do not have a team.

They will have the chance to substitute a player who has a team ONLY if there is one of those conditions:

  • 1 or more players of a team withdraws
  • 1 or more players of a team are disqualified

The whithdrawal of a player will be official when his absence/unavailability or uninterest are proved through screenshots to the @League admins.

There is no specific number of days after which the whithdrawal is automatically given: the admins will consider every situation.

A player who whithdraws or is disqualified CANNOT re-enter the league, therefore they will not be able to register as a Free agent either.

It is possible to for a team to pick a Free agent registered from at least 2 weeks or from a week earlier than the start of the league (29/03).

Once picked, the Free agent must join the team that called him and to do so within 3 days, penalty being the exclusion from the league.

7.1 Freecard

The freecard allows a Free agent to substitute an absent player for only a single match

Every team will have 1 Freecard to use for the first half of the season (the first 9 matches) and 1 Freecard for the second half (the last 9 matches).

Before being able to use it, it is needed to prove the absence/uninterest of the player to the @League admins through screenshots.

The Free agent called up through Freecard WILL NOT be able to be called up again from said team for the rest of the league (for example, in case the absence of the player continues after the match.)
